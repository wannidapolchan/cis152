One of my favorite drinks is Thai milk tea. Being Thai, this drink feels like home to me, and I love that I can easily make it. Its flavor combines a strong black tea base with spices, creamy sweetened condensed milk, and a vibrant orange color. I enjoy its sweet, refreshing, and slightly spicy taste, which stands out from other milk teas. I can easily make it at home using tea leaves, condensed milk, and a regular milk.
